About

Behind The Lens

I'm Jefferson Ooi, a photographer based in Malaysia with a passion for capturing authentic moments and meaningful stories.

My photography journey began with aviation photography and has gradually expanded into sports coverage, school events, cultural celebrations, and event documentation. Whether it's the excitement of a football match, the atmosphere of a community gathering, or the beauty of an aircraft in flight, I strive to preserve every moment through creative and thoughtful photography.

I enjoy capturing genuine emotions and natural interactions, creating images that feel real, timeless, and memorable.

Experience

My Journey

2025

Where It All Began

My photography journey started when I entered secondary school in 2025. My father, who previously worked as a journalist, had an old camera that was rarely used after smartphones became common.

Instead of letting the camera sit unused on a shelf, I chose to give it a second life through photography.

Rather than letting it remain forgotten, I decided to learn photography and make use of it. After joining my school's photography club, I began learning the fundamentals of photography, from camera settings and composition to capturing meaningful moments.

2025

First Photography Assignment

Not long after starting photography, I received my first assignment — covering a singing competition organized by a local primary school.

This opportunity gave me valuable real-world experience and marked the beginning of my journey into event photography.

2025 — Present

Building Experience Through Events

Since then, I have been fortunate to photograph a variety of events, including school activities, sports competitions, cultural celebrations, temple events, and community gatherings.

Each project has helped me improve my technical skills, storytelling ability, and confidence behind the camera.

2026 — Present

Documenting Communities & Traditions

In 2026, I began taking on more meaningful projects, including documenting temple restoration works, religious celebrations, Wesak Day events, and community activities.

Some projects also involved creating short documentary-style videos to preserve stories, traditions, and important moments for future generations.

Today

Photography continues to be both a passion and a way for me to contribute to my community. Through every assignment, I strive to create images that tell genuine stories and preserve memories that matter.
