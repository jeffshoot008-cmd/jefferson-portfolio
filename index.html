About

Behind the lens

I'm Jefferson, a student photographer passionate about capturing authentic moments. From the energy of a championship game to the quiet intensity of a portrait session, I approach every shoot with patience, creativity, and attention to detail.

My work spans school events, sports coverage, live performances, aviation photography, and professional portraits. I believe great photography isn't just about technical skill — it's about understanding people and telling their stories through light and composition.
